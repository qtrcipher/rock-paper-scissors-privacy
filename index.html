Privacy Policy for Rock Paper Scissors

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rock Paper Scissors handles information when you use the iOS app.

Contact

For privacy questions, account deletion, or data requests, contact: dev.hamam@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Offline gameplay works without an account. Offline match history and settings may be stored locally on your device.

Online gameplay requires Sign in with Apple through Firebase Authentication. We may store your Firebase user identifier, display name if provided, online matchmaking status, match results, aggregate stats, timestamps, and basic app settings.

Firebase Services

The app uses Firebase services for authentication, online matchmaking, database storage, cloud functions, remote configuration, analytics, crash reporting, and app integrity protection. Firebase may process device, usage, diagnostic, and app interaction data according to Google Firebase policies.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, ad interactions, and diagnostics depending on your device settings, consent choices, and applicable law. Ads are not intended to interrupt active gameplay.

Audio Assets

Game music and sound effects may be generated with ElevenLabs during development and bundled with the app. The app does not send your voice, audio, prompts, or gameplay data to ElevenLabs at runtime.

How We Use Information

  • Provide offline and online gameplay.
  • Match online players and resolve match results.
  • Save stats, settings, and account state.
  • Monitor reliability, crashes, and app performance.
  • Show and measure ads.

Data Sharing

We use third-party service providers including Apple, Google Firebase, Google AdMob, and ElevenLabs for the purposes described above. We do not sell personal information.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your online account data by emailing dev.hamam@gmail.com. Local offline data can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. Updates will be published on this page with a new effective date.
