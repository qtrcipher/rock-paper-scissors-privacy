Privacy Policy for Rock Paper Scissors

Effective date: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rock Paper Scissors handles information when you use the iOS app. The current release provides offline gameplay, local match history, Game Center achievements, ads, and an optional remove-ads purchase.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: dev.hamam@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

The app does not create an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, audio, or precise location.

Offline match history, language, settings, and purchase state may be stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes local app data from the device.

Firebase Services

The app uses Firebase services for app configuration, analytics, crash reporting, and app integrity protection. Firebase may process app instance identifiers, device information, usage events, diagnostics, crash logs, and performance data according to Google Firebase policies.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. The app requests non-personalized ads. AdMob may still process device information, ad interactions, diagnostics, and approximate location derived from network information to deliver, limit, and measure ads.

Purchases

The app offers an optional remove-ads purchase through Apple's in-app purchase system. Apple processes payment information. We do not receive your payment card details.

Game Center

If you use Apple Game Center, the app may report achievement progress to Apple. Apple manages your Game Center account and related profile data.

Audio Assets

Game music and sound effects may be generated with ElevenLabs during development and bundled with the app. The app does not send your voice, audio, prompts, or gameplay data to ElevenLabs at runtime.

How We Use Information

  • Provide offline gameplay, match history, settings, and achievements.
  • Maintain purchase state and remove ads after purchase.
  • Monitor reliability, crashes, and app performance.
  • Show, limit, and measure ads.

Data Sharing

We use third-party service providers including Apple, Google Firebase, and Google AdMob for the purposes described above. We do not sell personal information.

Tracking

The current release does not request App Tracking Transparency permission and does not use data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Deletion

Local offline data can be removed by deleting the app from your device. For questions about data handled by Apple, Google Firebase, or Google AdMob, review those providers' privacy controls and policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. Updates will be published on this page with a new effective date.
